FAQ

Is this product safe for sensitive skin?

Yes, this product is designed for regular application on sensitive skin. However, we recommend familiarising yourself with the ingredients and avoiding use of this product if it contains anything you’re allergic to. We also recommend doing a patch test on your inner wrist before using on the lips.

Can I use this balm under my eyes?

Yes, this balm is multifunctional and can be used on the skin around the eyes. Press and dab balm gently in a stippling motion on the skin around the eyes. Avoid contact with the eye and eyelashes, as direct contact with the eye may result in stinging.

Can I use this balm if I’m pregnant?

There can be an increase in skin sensitivity during pregnancy, so you may want to consult a healthcare professional before use. This balm contains Castor Oil, which some expectant mothers may have increased skin sensitivity to during pregnancy. Castor Oil can trigger muscle cramps and potentially induce labour in pregnant mothers. It is not recommended to consume Castor Oil during pregnancy. Avoid direct ingestion of Castor Oil if pregnant. Ingredients such as Hyaluronic Acid, Vitamin B3 & Vitamin B5 are generally considered safe for topical use during pregnancy.

Can I use this balm if I just had lip filler injections?

Dermatologists recommend avoiding the use of cosmetic products on or around the mouth for at least 24 hours after treatment. An increase in swelling and skin sensitivity may lead to having an uncommon adverse reaction to any cosmetic product during that period. If your swelling has significantly reduced after that time, you may proceed to apply this balm cautiously. If your skin is still too sensitive for application, you can wash the balm off with a gentle soap and lukewarm water.

How do I dispose of the empty package responsibly?

You can dispose of the Kraft paper package by placing it in your compost bin or in the non-recyclable waste bin, as it is readily biodegradable and will decompose naturally when exposed to the elements.
